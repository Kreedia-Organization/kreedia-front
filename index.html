How It Works

4 simple steps to transform your environmental actions into crypto rewards and NFTs

Spot and Photograph

Find a dirty spot in your city and take a geolocated photo to report the problem.

NGO Validation

Our NGO partners verify and validate your report to ensure its authenticity.

Clean and Prove

Clean the reported area and submit an "after" photo to prove your positive action.

Receive Your Rewards

Earn on Base L2 and get a unique NFT as a badge of your environmental impact.
